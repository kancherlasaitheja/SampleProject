About

Anime rankings for monthly discovery

Anime Sanyasi is a small anime discovery site focused on monthly top 10 watchlists. The goal is to help viewers quickly understand what is worth watching this month and why.

Rankings are editorial. They use publicly available seasonal data, current audience signals, release timing, and original commentary. They are not official awards, studio endorsements, or streaming availability guarantees.

Update rhythm

The homepage is designed to refresh monthly. Each update should replace the month, source data, ranking notes, and last-updated date.
