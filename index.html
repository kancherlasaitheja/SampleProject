Method

How this list was built

This is an editorial watchlist, not an official awards result. The July list uses Summer 2026 seasonal data from MyAnimeList through the public Jikan API, then balances score, member count, premiere timing, and franchise momentum. Titles that have not aired yet are clearly marked instead of being assigned fake scores.

This approach keeps the page useful early in the month while still making it easy to refresh when more episodes and better audience data become available.
